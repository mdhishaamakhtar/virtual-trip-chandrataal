# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

One person: Vaniya Adeeb. A young woman in her early twenties from Lucknow — the reader, the subject, and the protagonist. She encounters this site as a personal artifact made for her. She is literary, introspective, deeply ambitious. She recognizes herself in the story without being told it is her.

## Product Purpose

An immersive editorial storytelling website that recreates a solo mountain trip from Lucknow to Chandra Taal lake in Spiti Valley, told in six chapters. Each chapter is a full-screen cinematic experience — one paragraph revealed at a time. The site is a gift. It should feel like opening something handmade, not loading a webpage.

## Brand Personality

Intimate. Literary. Expansive. The voice of a long letter written to someone you know completely. Not a travel site. Not a journal. Something between a poem and a film.

## Anti-references

- Travel blogs: listicles, stock photos, banner ads, "10 things to do in Spiti"
- Dark outdoorsy adventure brands: REI aesthetic, bold sporty typography, high-contrast hero overlays with "EXPLORE NOW"
- Minimalist Apple-style whitespace: this story is textured and dense and layered, not spare

## Design Principles

1. **The story IS the interface.** No chrome competes with the words. Navigation is invisible until needed.
2. **Each chapter earns its own world.** Color, texture, sound, and image are not decoration — they are the place.
3. **Pacing is sacred.** Cinematic paragraph-by-paragraph reveal. The reader moves at the story's speed.
4. **Nothing generic.** Every visual choice should be specific enough that it could only be this story.
5. **Made for one.** The intimacy of something personal — not broadcast design, not a product for users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast. Respects prefers-reduced-motion. Mobile-first layout.
